About Me

After getting laid off from a marketing role I essentially "fell" into, I realized that I needed a change of scenery, and moreso, a complete career change. Ever since I was a child spending my summer vacations tinkering with my Neopets page and redesigining my MySpace profile, I always had an affinity for code and web design. My career as a content marketer rekindled that, as I was occasionally tasked with handling some CSS and HTML and it was always something I enjoyed digging into. After many years feeling out of place at work, and in the "wrong" place, I decided to take the leap and use some newly-found free time to learn to program from scratch.

Skills: HTML/CSS, JavaScript, C, Python, Node.js, Express, SQL, MongoDB, Postman, React
